# Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you. Whether you have a question, suggestion, or just want to say hello, feel free to reach out.

## Email

Send us an email at **admin@techcompare.dev** and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within **24–48 hours** during business days. For urgent matters, please indicate so in your subject line.

## Get in Touch

When contacting us, please include:

- Your name
- A brief message or question
- Any relevant context (e.g., page URL, product name)

We read every message and value your feedback.
